We Distribute to the Following Brands

Eezi Quartz

Eezi Quartz offers an affordable alternative to wood, laminates and scratch-easy surfaces, by offering high-quality and beautiful quartz countertops and slabs.

Caesarstone

The original, premium quartz surface, Caesarstone oozes sophistication and elegance. Caesarstone quartz surfaces are ideal for virtually any interior surface from kitchen countertops, bathroom vanities and stairs, to wall panelling and interior furniture.

Infinity

Infinity specializes in the production of high quality large-size porcelain slabs for architecture, interiors and tailor-made solutions such as kitchens, bathrooms and bars.

Lutum

LUTUM porcelain offers perfectly level, large-scale slabs for both inside and out. Future-focused and uncompromising, LUTUM is setting the trend for what is to come in designer spaces.

Rudi's Choice

Each one is packed with natural stone countertops, worktops and slabs, that have been hand-selected from across the globe by Rudi Eggers, International Slab Sales' expert CEO.
